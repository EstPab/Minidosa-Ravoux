Maintenance

Preventive maintenance

Draining the hoses

The hose under the tank must be connected to the tank.

The valve under the tank must be closed.

  1. Place a container under the filling station.
  2. Turn the AUTO / O / MAIN selector switch to the O position.
  3. Press the PURGE button as many times as necessary.

When the hoses are empty, no liquid enters the syringe of the filling station.

Drain the tank

The hoses must be drained, .

The valve must be closed.

  1. Disconnect the hose under the tank.
  2. Place a container under the valve of the tank.
  3. Open the valve under the tank so that the liquid flows into the container.

Clean the hoses

The hoses must be drained,.

  1. Fill the tank with clean water.
  2. Drain the hoses again ().

Corrective maintenance

Replace the bulb of the EN SERVICE (In service) illuminated button

The machine must be powered off.

  1. Unscrew the transparent cap.
  2. Unscrew the colored cap.
  3. Press and turn a finger placed on the bulb to unscrew it.
  4. Press and turn a finger placed on the new bulb to screw it in.
  5. Screw the green cap back on.
  6. Screw the transparent cap back on.

A white plastic disc is placed inside the colored cap.

The caps are fragile; screw carefully.

Replace the bulb of the DEFAUT (Default) indicator light

The machine must be powered off.

  1. Unscrew the colored cap.
  2. Remove the bulb.
  3. Install the new bulb.
  4. Screw the colored cap back on.

A white plastic disc is placed inside the colored cap.

The caps are fragile; screw carefully.

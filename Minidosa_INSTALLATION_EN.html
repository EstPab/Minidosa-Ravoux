Installation

Installation instructions

Position the MINIDOSA machine in accordance with the following characteristics.

Connections 230 V.
Required clearance space Width: 115 cm (45.3 in) Depth: 75 cm (29.5 in) Height: 120 cm (47.2 in)
Installation height The machine can be placed on the support legs or on another surface. The support legs can be adjusted. Suggested minimum height for the support legs or the other surface: 100 cm (39.4 in).
Clearance around the machine Perimeter of 50 cm (19.7 in) around the machine.
Installation support Flat, stable and level surface capable of supporting 150 kg (331 lb).
Distance between the machine and the air and electrical outlets Less than 200 cm (78.7 in).

Connecting the machine to the power supplies

Connecting the machine to the electrical supply.

The machine must be installed on a flat and stable surface capable of supporting 150 kg (331 lb).

  1. Plug the electrical cable into a 230 V power outlet.

Connecting the machine to the pneumatic supply

The machine must be installed on a flat and stable surface capable of supporting 150 kg (331 lb).

  1. Connect the pneumatic hose to an air supply with a pressure higher than 6 bar (87 psi).
